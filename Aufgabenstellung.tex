\section{Scope of this Thesis}
\vspace{0.3\textheight}
\begin{justify}
This thesis aims to study the non-abelian exchange of Majorana modes in the 2D chiral topological p-Wave superconductor vortices to observe the quasi-particle exchange during adiabatic evolution in real space and to probe the exchange in the non adiabatic regime for the use of quantum computation. Additionally, the effect of different parameter and vortex geometry choices will be explored.
\end{justify}
